**Short answer:**  
The key terms for **Amazon Connect Customer** (the modern name for the Amazon Connect contact‑centre platform) fall into five major groups: **omnichannel communication**, **routing & flows**, **AI/automation**, **agent productivity**, and **analytics/operations**. Below is a complete, structured list synthesized from the official AWS documentation.  [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/what-is-amazon-connect.md)  [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/connect-feature-overview.html)  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/documentation-overview/connect-customer/)

---

## 📘 Core Amazon Connect Customer Key Terms  




### 🟦 Omnichannel Communication
- **Voice** — High‑quality 16kHz softphone audio, DID/toll‑free numbers, global telephony.  [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/connect-feature-overview.html)  
- **Chat** — Web chat, mobile chat, persistent transcripts.  
- **SMS** — Text messaging support.  
- **Web calling / Video** — In‑app calling, video, screen share.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/documentation-overview/connect-customer/)  
- **Email** — Routing, prioritisation, automated workflows.  
- **Tasks** — Unified task management across channels.

---

### 🟩 Routing, Flows & Contact Control
- **Flows** — Drag‑and‑drop workflow designer for IVR, chatbots, agent guidance.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/documentation-overview/connect-customer/)  
- **Queues** — Customer waiting lines with routing logic.  
- **Routing Profiles** — Determines which agents receive which contacts.  
- **Contact Lens** — Conversation analytics, sentiment, compliance.  
- **Customer Profiles** — Unified customer data across interactions.  
- **Hours of Operation** — Time‑based routing rules.  
- **Phone Numbers** — DID, toll‑free, outbound numbers.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/products/connect/customer/features/)

---

### 🟨 AI, Automation & Self‑Service
- **Amazon Lex** — Conversational IVR/chatbots with NLU/ASR.  [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/connect-feature-overview.html)  
- **Agentic AI** — Autonomous AI agents that resolve issues, carry context across channels.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/products/connect/customer/)  
- **Generative AI features** — LLM‑assisted bot creation, slot resolution, FAQs.  
- **Live Sync** — Visual step‑through guidance (preview).  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/products/connect/customer/)  
- **Lambda Integrations** — Custom logic inside flows.

---

### 🟧 Agent Experience & Productivity
- **Agent Workspace** — Unified UI for voice, chat, tasks, customer profile.  
- **Softphone** — High‑quality browser‑based calling.  [AWS Documentation](https://docs.aws.amazon.com/connect/latest/adminguide/connect-feature-overview.html)  
- **Guided Assistance** — Step‑by‑step agent workflows.  
- **After‑call work automation** — AI‑assisted wrap‑up tasks.  
- **Screen Share** — Agent view of customer screen for troubleshooting.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/documentation-overview/connect-customer/)

---

### 🟥 Analytics, Insights & Operations
- **Real‑time Metrics** — Live dashboards for queues, agents, contacts.  
- **Historical Metrics** — Reporting on past interactions.  
- **Contact Trace Records (CTR)** — Detailed logs of every interaction.  
- **Quality Management** — Evaluation forms, scoring.  
- **Forecasting & Scheduling** — Workforce management.  
- **Rules Engine** — Event‑driven automation and alerts.

---

### 🟪 Administration & Integration
- **Instance** — The core Connect environment.  
- **Security Profiles** — Role‑based access control.  
- **Users & Agents** — Identity and permissions.  
- **Telephony Providers** — Global carrier network.  [AWS](https://aws.amazon.com/products/connect/customer/features/)  
- **API & SDK** — Programmatic access for flows, routing, metrics.  
- **Integrations** — CRM, AWS services, third‑party systems.
